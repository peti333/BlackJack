Blackjack Rules

Objective

The goal of Blackjack is to beat the dealer by getting a hand total as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it. Face cards are worth 10, aces can be worth 1 or 11, and all other cards are worth their face value.

Steps of the Game

1. Betting

After joining a room, each player places a bet by selecting an amount and clicking on the circle to add it to their current bet.

2. Dealing

Once the game begins, each player and the dealer receive two cards. One of the dealer's cards remains hidden from players.

3. Player Actions

Players take turns to choose one of the following actions in the order they joined:

  • Hit: Draw an additional card to increase the hand total. Going over 21 will result in a bust, meaning you lose immediately.
  • Stand: Keep your hand and end your turn.
  • Double Down: Double your original bet and draw one more card. You cannot ask for any more cards and your turn is over.
  • Split: If your first two cards are of the same value, you can split them into two separate hands, each hand having half of your original bet's value. You then play each hand separately.

4. Dealer's Turn

After all players have completed their actions, the dealer reveals their hidden card and plays according to standard rules. The dealer must hit until their total is 17 or higher, and will stand on a soft 17 or more.

5. Results

Once the dealer finishes, the hands are compared:

  • If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer’s hand without busting, you win.
  • If you bust (go over 21) or have a lower hand than the dealer, you lose.
  • If you and the dealer have the same hand total, it’s a tie, and your bet is returned.
  • If the dealer busts, any remaining players win.

Additional Rules

  • Blackjack: A natural 21 (an Ace and a 10-value card) on your first two cards pays out at 3:2 unless the dealer also has a Blackjack, in which case it’s a tie.
  • Five-Card Charlie: If a player manages to have 5 cards in their hand without the sum of the values of the cards not exceding 21, the player automaticly wins.
